His life
Thomas Finney was born on April 5 1922 at Preston, within earshot of the Deepdale ground.
His father, Alf, worked occasionally as a clerk but was often unemployed.
One of six children, young Tom grew up in poverty made harder by the death of his mother when he was just four.
